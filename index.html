Singapore

Gardens by the bay

One of the most touristic areas to visit is the Gardens by the bay. This botanical garden is absolutely magical and is home to over 4,00,000 plants. What also makes this garden unique is the fact that the structure of the garden is very futuristic, and there is even a waterfall in the middle of it!

Lazarus Island

If you want to have relaxed beach day Lazarus Island is the perfect spot! The sandy beach and azure blue sea is perfect for relaxing after enjoying the bustling city life.

Universal Studios Singapore

Who doesn't like a good amusement park to be able to have fun with your family? The Universal Studios in Singapore is perfect and almost rivals the one is the USA! With its 28 rides and exciting shows this is the perfect activity to have an exhilirating day.

River Safari Singapore

This activity is a river themed zoo and aquarium! This reserve is home to 6,000 animals of over 300 different species that you can see all while knowing that they are taking care of very well and do not face animal cruelty.

ArtScience Museum

Another great thing you cannot miss in Singapore is the ArtScience Museum. This museum includes a variety of exhibitions in the fiels of technology, art, media and so much more. With its permanent exhibit FUTURE WORLD: Where art meets science and dozens of new local and international exhbitis you could never get bored there!

VivoCity

Shopping is another essential thing to do when traveling and in Singapore the largest mall there is is the VivoCity mall. With the largest cinema in the city, wade pool and dozens of stores at your fingertips this shopping area is absolutely perfect for people of all ages!
